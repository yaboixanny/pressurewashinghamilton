Common Questions

Commercial Pressure Washing FAQs

What commercial pressure washing services do you offer? expand_more
We offer comprehensive commercial pressure washing including parking lot cleaning, building exterior washing, sidewalk and walkway cleaning, loading dock cleaning, dumpster pad cleaning, graffiti removal, and fleet washing. We serve retail plazas, office buildings, restaurants, warehouses, and industrial facilities.
Do you offer night and weekend commercial cleaning? expand_more
Yes! We understand that business operations come first. We offer flexible scheduling including overnight cleaning, early morning service, and weekend appointments to minimize disruption to your customers and employees.
Are you insured for commercial work? expand_more
Yes, we carry $5 million in commercial liability insurance and are fully WSIB compliant. We can provide certificates of insurance to property managers and building owners upon request.
Do you offer commercial maintenance contracts? expand_more
Yes! We offer ongoing maintenance contracts with scheduled cleanings at discounted rates. Regular maintenance keeps your property looking professional year-round, improves safety, and can reduce long-term cleaning costs.
How do you handle environmental concerns? expand_more
We follow all local environmental regulations for commercial pressure washing. We use eco-friendly cleaning solutions and can implement water recovery systems when required. Our team is trained in proper wastewater management.
