FAQs

Common Questions About Storefront Cleaning

How much does storefront cleaning cost in Hamilton? expand_more
Storefront cleaning in Hamilton typically ranges from $150 to $500+ depending on the size, height, and condition of your storefront. We provide free on-site assessments and detailed quotes for all commercial properties.
How often should I have my storefront cleaned? expand_more
We recommend professional storefront cleaning every 1-3 months for high-traffic retail locations, and quarterly for most businesses. Regular cleaning maintains curb appeal and prevents buildup that can damage surfaces over time.
Do you clean windows as part of storefront cleaning? expand_more
Yes! Our storefront cleaning service includes exterior window cleaning, building facades, awnings, signage, sidewalks, and entryways. We provide a complete storefront refresh to maximize your curb appeal.
Can you clean storefronts early morning or after hours? expand_more
Absolutely! We offer flexible scheduling including early morning, evening, and weekend appointments to minimize disruption to your business operations and customers.
What types of storefronts do you clean? expand_more
We clean all types of commercial storefronts including retail stores, restaurants, cafes, salons, banks, medical offices, and professional services. Our team is experienced with various building materials and facade types.
