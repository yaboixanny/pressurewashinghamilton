Driveway Cleaning FAQs

How much does driveway cleaning cost in Hamilton? expand_more
Driveway cleaning in Hamilton typically costs between $150 and $400 depending on the size of your driveway, the surface type, and level of staining. We provide free quotes so you know exactly what to expect.
Can pressure washing remove oil stains from my driveway? expand_more
Yes! Our high-pressure cleaning combined with specialized degreasing agents can remove most oil stains from concrete and paver driveways. Fresh stains are easier to remove, but we can significantly improve even older, set-in stains.
Is pressure washing safe for interlock and paver driveways? expand_more
Absolutely. We adjust our pressure settings and techniques based on your driveway surface. For interlock and pavers, we use appropriate pressure levels that clean effectively without damaging the stones or displacing the joint sand.
How often should I have my driveway cleaned? expand_more
We recommend professional driveway cleaning every 1-2 years in Hamilton. However, driveways with heavy vehicle traffic, tree coverage, or persistent staining may benefit from annual cleaning.
How long does driveway pressure washing take? expand_more
Most residential driveway cleaning jobs take between 1-2 hours depending on the size and condition of your driveway. Larger driveways or those with heavy staining may take longer. We'll provide a time estimate with your free quote.
