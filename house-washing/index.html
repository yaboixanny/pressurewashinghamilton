Expert House Cleaning

Professional House Washing for Hamilton Homes

House washing is a professional exterior cleaning service designed to safely remove years of dirt, grime, mold, mildew, and algae from your home's siding. Over time, Ontario's humid climate causes organic growth to accumulate on exterior surfaces, making your home look aged and potentially causing long-term damage.

Our Hamilton house washing service uses a technique called soft washing—a low-pressure cleaning method combined with specialized, eco-friendly cleaning solutions. Unlike traditional pressure washing, soft washing is gentle enough for delicate surfaces while being powerful enough to eliminate stubborn stains and biological growth.

Common Questions

House Washing FAQs

Is house washing safe for vinyl siding? expand_more
Yes! Our soft wash technique uses low-pressure water combined with specialized cleaning solutions that are completely safe for vinyl siding. Unlike high-pressure washing, soft washing won't crack, warp, or damage your siding.
How often should I wash my house in Ontario? expand_more
We recommend house washing every 1-2 years in Hamilton and the surrounding Ontario area. Homes with more shade or near trees may need cleaning more frequently due to increased mold and algae growth.
Do you use high pressure on house siding? expand_more
No, we use a soft wash method for all house washing services. This means low-pressure water combined with eco-friendly cleaning solutions that safely remove dirt, mold, and algae without risking damage to your home's exterior.
How long does house washing take? expand_more
Most residential house washing jobs in Hamilton take between 1-3 hours depending on the size of your home and level of buildup. We'll provide a time estimate during your free quote.
Will house washing remove mold and algae? expand_more
Absolutely! Our soft wash solutions are specifically designed to kill and remove mold, mildew, algae, and other organic growth from your home's exterior. The results are immediate and long-lasting.
