Deck Cleaning FAQs

How much does deck cleaning cost in Hamilton? expand_more
Deck cleaning in Hamilton typically costs between $150 and $400 depending on the size of your deck, the material type, and level of buildup. We provide free quotes so you know exactly what to expect before we start.
Is pressure washing safe for wood decks? expand_more
Yes, when done correctly. We use adjustable pressure settings and proper techniques specifically designed for wood decks. Too much pressure can damage wood fibers, which is why we use a controlled approach that cleans effectively without causing splintering or etching.
Can you clean composite and Trex decks? expand_more
Absolutely! We clean all types of composite decking including Trex, TimberTech, and Fiberon. We use manufacturer-recommended cleaning methods and lower pressure settings to safely clean composite materials without voiding warranties.
How often should I have my deck professionally cleaned? expand_more
We recommend professional deck cleaning every 1-2 years in Hamilton. Decks with heavy shade, near trees, or in damp areas may benefit from annual cleaning to prevent mold and algae buildup.
Do you offer deck staining and sealing after cleaning? expand_more
Yes! We offer complete deck restoration services including cleaning, brightening, staining, and sealing. A freshly cleaned deck is the perfect canvas for stain or sealant application. Ask us about our deck restoration packages.
