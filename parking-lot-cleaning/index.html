Parking Lot Cleaning FAQs

How much does parking lot cleaning cost in Hamilton? expand_more
Commercial parking lot cleaning in Hamilton typically ranges from $0.08 to $0.15 per square foot, or $500 to $2,500+ depending on the lot size and condition. We provide free on-site assessments and detailed quotes for all commercial properties.
Can you clean parking lots at night to avoid disrupting business? expand_more
Yes! We offer flexible scheduling including nights, weekends, and early mornings to minimize disruption to your business operations. Many retail and commercial clients prefer overnight cleaning when their lots are empty.
What types of stains can you remove from parking lots? expand_more
Our commercial pressure washing removes oil and grease stains, tire marks, chewing gum, paint markings, food and beverage spills, rust stains, and general dirt and grime buildup. We use specialized degreasers for tough automotive fluids.
How often should commercial parking lots be cleaned? expand_more
We recommend professional parking lot cleaning 2-4 times per year for most commercial properties. High-traffic locations like fast food restaurants, gas stations, and busy retail plazas may benefit from monthly or quarterly cleaning.
Do you offer parking lot maintenance contracts? expand_more
Yes! We offer ongoing maintenance contracts with scheduled cleanings at discounted rates. Regular maintenance keeps your property looking professional year-round and can reduce long-term cleaning costs.
